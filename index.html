Lone Wolf

Today was my first 'lone tourist' day probably since I've arrived here, which sounds ridiculous, but I really haven't had the chance. Having the chance to be alone and listen to music and walk around one of most beautiful cities in the world, with my handy camera, was so soothing really. God my legs hurt and my back was hurting from carrying so much crap in my backpack. But at the end of the day i found a massive vegan donut and isn't that the moral desssert we are all hoping for.
